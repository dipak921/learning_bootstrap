About Us

Jain Social Federation's Anandrishiji Hospital and Medical Research Center stands as a beacon of advanced medical care and community-focused health services in Ahmednagar, Maharashtra. Since its establishment, the hospital has dedicated itself to enhancing patient care through innovative practices and a comprehensive healthcare delivery system.Located on Anandrishiji Marg, the hospital has made significant strides in providing high-quality medical services. It manages a substantial patient volume, with 242,139 in-patients and 1,481,597 out-patients served to date. Additionally, the hospital has conducted 47,001 surgeries and has achieved an impressive 96% in-patient satisfaction rate, reflecting its commitment to excellent care.The facility is equipped with a range of modern services and specializations, including cardiology, nephrology, and pediatrics, supported by a team of highly qualified super-specialty doctors. Anandrishiji Hospital is known for its state-of-the-art dialysis center, ICU, and comprehensive diagnostic services. For those seeking high-quality medical care, Anandrishiji Hospital offers a patient portal for easy access to health reports and maintains a 24-hour emergency service. For more information or to book an appointment, individuals are encouraged to contact the hospital directly at their provided numbers.
