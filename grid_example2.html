Blood collection tubes

Blood collection tubes, also known as vacutainers, are specialized tubes used to collect blood samples for laboratory testing. They come in various types, each with a specific color-coded cap and additive, designed for different tests. Understanding the purpose of each tube is crucial for accurate sample collection and reliable test results

Injections

Injections deliver liquid medications, fluids, or nutrients directly into a person's body. Different types of injections include intravenous, intramuscular, subcutaneous, intraosseous, and intradermal.

Blood money

Blood money" generally refers to financial compensation paid to the family of a murder victim, or money obtained from a crime, especially at the cost of another's life. It can also refer to money received for providing information leading to the arrest of a criminal. In the context of clinical trials, "blood money" can describe the money paid to participants, which can lead to individuals over-volunteering and potentially jeopardizing their health.
